This is the college site.

Yes. The definitive college site you've been waiting for. A site built to help you with the college process that's completely free of clutter, and looks like it was built in this decade. Oh, and we almost forgot. It's a site made by college students for future college students. Look no further. Welcome home.

Join the revolution! Current college student? Get verified!

This site was founded on 3 core principles.

  • You should get college advice from college students. Not strangers on the Internet.
  • You should have access to a site that looks and feels modern, clean, and easy to use. It should be easy to use on phones.
  • You should have a reliable source of interesting content, relevant and informative statistics, and solid advice. We can be your ground control.

That's what sparked this project. Two friends who were tired of seeing students try to navigate through the admissions process without an accessible and visually appealing platform to come back to again and again. Welcome to ForSure. We're glad to have you.
